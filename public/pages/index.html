RetireCalc

Simple calculator that will calculate how much retirement money you will have based on yearly deposits for a certain number of years at a specific interest rate. Based on the retirement money, interest rate after retirement, and number of years after retirement, the calculator will return how much you can withdraw yearly after retirement.

You can also individually calculate the future value of an annuity and the present value of an annuity.
